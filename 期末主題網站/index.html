gap year

more information about gap year

advantages

1.career plan

You can use more time to plan your life also find your other potential ability or learn some new skills

 

2.life experience

During this additional year, you can choose to travel around or take an intership. NO matter what option you choose, which will become some useful life experiences in your life.

3.reflection

In this fast-pace world, we all need some time to spend with ourselves, knowing what really matter to us

disadvantages

1. an expensive year

while investing in yourself, these courses or internship will cost lots of money

2.hard to keep in touch with friends

It is hard to contact with friends as frequently as past when you are all busy in life or works.

3.burden social preesure

Maybe some of your families or friends won't show their supports or understandings to your desicion,you still need to strengthen your mind and stand for yourself.
